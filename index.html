About 100

100 Projects Media is a South African cultural media platform focused on fashion, music, sport, business and community. Through 100 Magazine, 100 Conversations, events and multimedia production, we create stories that connect brands with culture-first audiences.

Our work spans editorial publishing, photography, film, podcasts, live experiences and strategic content distribution across digital platforms.
